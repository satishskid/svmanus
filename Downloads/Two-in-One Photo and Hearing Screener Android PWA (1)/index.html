User Guide

üì• Getting Started

  1. Access the App: Visit the app URL or install it as a PWA on your device
  2. Log In: Sign in with your email address
  3. Check Access: If your email is whitelisted, you'll have immediate access
  4. Select Screening: Choose Vision, Hearing, or both from the home page

üëÅÔ∏è Vision Screening Guide

  1. Photoscreening:
    • Allow camera access when prompted
    • Position your face in front of the camera
    • The app will detect red reflex and eye alignment
    • Results show normal, abnormal, or unclear findings
  2. Visual Acuity Test:
    • Sit at arm's length from your device (approximately 50cm)
    • Cover one eye and read the letters/symbols
    • Start from the largest line and work downward
    • Continue until you can no longer read clearly
    • Repeat for the other eye

üëÇ Hearing Screening Guide

  1. Preparation:
    • Use headphones or earbuds for best results
    • Find a quiet environment
    • Adjust volume to a comfortable level during calibration
  2. Pure Tone Audiometry:
    • Listen for tones at different frequencies
    • Click "I Heard It" when you detect a tone
    • The app will adjust volume automatically
    • Tests both ears at 6 different frequencies
  3. Speech-in-Noise Test:
    • Listen to words spoken in background noise
    • Select the word you heard from multiple choices
    • Simulates real-world listening conditions

üìä Understanding Your Results

  1. Vision Results:
    • Pass: Normal findings, no concerns
    • Refer: Abnormal findings, consult an eye care professional
    • Inconclusive: Unclear results, consider retesting or professional evaluation
  2. Hearing Results:
    • Thresholds: Measured in dB HL (Hearing Level)
    • Normal: Thresholds ‚â§ 25 dB HL
    • Mild Loss: Thresholds 26-40 dB HL
    • Moderate Loss: Thresholds > 40 dB HL

‚ö†Ô∏è When to Seek Professional Help

  • Any abnormal or inconclusive vision screening results
  • Hearing thresholds above 25 dB HL
  • Difficulty understanding speech in noisy environments
  • Any concerns about your vision or hearing
  • If results don't match your expectations
